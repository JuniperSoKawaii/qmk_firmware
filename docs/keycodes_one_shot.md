|Key         |Description                       |
|------------|----------------------------------|
|`OSM(mod)`  |Hold `mod` for one keypress       |
|`OSL(layer)`|Switch to `layer` for one keypress|
|`OS_ON`     |Turns One Shot keys on            |
|`OS_OFF`    |Turns One Shot keys off           |
|`OS_TOGG`   |Toggles One Shot keys status      |
